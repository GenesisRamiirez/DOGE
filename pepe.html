Welcome to Pepe Cardinals!

We are a team of passionate bird lovers dedicated to providing high-quality care for our feathered friends. We offer a variety of services, including boarding, grooming, and training. We also have a wide selection of bird supplies for sale.

About Us

Pepe Cardinals was founded in 2010 by two friends who shared a love of birds. We are committed to providing the best possible care for our birds and we are always learning new ways to improve our services.

We are a small, family-owned business and we take pride in our work. We are passionate about birds and we are committed to providing our clients with the best possible experience.
